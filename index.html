About Jane

Jane is originally from Yorkshire in the United Kingdom, but now resides in Vancouver, BC.

She has extensive experience working in the arts sector, having worked in and operated box offices and managed ticketing systems across numerous venues and large-scale events since 2008. Wrangling ticketing systems led to an interest in databases and data analytics, while the nature of working in under-resourced charitable organizations meant she also had the opportunity to develop skills in digital marketing and web development.

Now studying towards a Bachelor of Science in Applied Computer Science at Dominican University of California, Jane is a Dev Degree intern at Shopify, looking to expand her understanding of systems and her love of creative problem solving.
